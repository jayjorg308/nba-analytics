# Zone details are a click-opened overlay; hover is affordance only

Zone details open as a click/tap-opened card (`ZoneDetailCard`) covering the chart wrapper — on **every** device, one interaction model, not a desktop-hover/mobile-tap fork. Hovering a zone is a visual affordance only (cursor + brightness, gated behind `@media (hover: hover)` so touch never sticks); each zone `<g>` is a real button (role, tabindex, Enter/Space), and the card dismisses via its close button, Escape, or the view toggle, returning focus to the zone that opened it. The Shots view keeps its hover tooltip but ignores non-mouse pointers — on touch the scatter is view-only. Both court SVGs carry `touch-action: manipulation` and tap-highlight/selection suppression.

**Why:** hover is invisible to touch (ADR-0018 said it; this follows through). The pointerenter-driven tooltip was broken on phones three ways at once: a tap fires `pointerenter` with no `pointerleave` until the next tap (tooltips stuck), the nowrap tooltip clamped only its horizontal center (it clipped off-screen at the court's top and edges), and nothing suppressed double-tap zoom (the whole chart lurched on tap). A card also fits the content: the zone story now carries the diet share and the making-scale position alongside FG%/PPS — more than a cursor-anchored box can hold legibly. The overlay covers the court rather than pushing it because a toggle-opened panel in flow would shift the page (ADR-0026's companion invariant). Per-shot detail on touch was deliberately dropped, not ported: the scatter is the secondary look-closer view and the zone table remains the accessible/touch data twin — FGA and the † flag stay in the table regardless, never overlay-only.

**Consequences:** the zones svg is a labeled `group` of zone buttons, not a labeled image (interactive children are invalid inside `role="img"`; the ShotChart keeps image semantics). The card only re-presents `ZoneMetricsRow` through `src/format.ts` (ADR-0011), keyed by the clicked zone's name, never by tap coordinates (ADR-0012); its Making Δ renders beside its two FG% anchors, so it is their displayed gap (`formatSignedGap`, ADR-0023) — the old tooltip rounded the raw delta beside the same anchors, and that form must not return. The mini scale reuses the fixed bins and `--making-*` variables with no text on fills (ADR-0013/0014). The card is not a focus trap and not `aria-modal` — a local disclosure, Tab exits naturally. Enter animation honors `prefers-reduced-motion`; exit is instant by design (keyframe-on-mount keeps the component stateless — a slide-out would need an exiting-state render pass). The shot tooltip now measures itself and clamps inside the wrapper on both axes, flipping below the dot at the top edge.
