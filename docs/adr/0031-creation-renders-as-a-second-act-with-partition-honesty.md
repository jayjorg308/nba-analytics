# Creation renders as a second act, families render whole, and no new scales ship

The creation section is the hero page's **second act**: it sits after the court and zone table, under the section-title recipe (`SHOT CREATION` + a one-line description stating the comparison class), mirroring the `hero-layout` pattern — a hand-rolled SVG **creation-diet chart** (paired horizontal bars per context: his attempt share vs the league's, the two families as stacked groups, neutral chart ink) beside a **creation table** in the zone table's column philosophy (`Context / FGA / Share / Lg share / PPS (lg)` — payoff first, reference trailing, FGA always visible as the honesty anchor). Every context of a shipped family always renders, and conversion carries the same `< 50 attempts` † flag constant as the rest of the page.

**Why second act, not third headline:** the verdict now argues on two levels — the two-axis thesis and the creation why (ADR-0029) — and the evidence unfolds in the same order the verdict argues: court + table back the thesis first, then the creation section backs the why. Wedging creation between the headline cards and the court would put second-topic evidence in front of the primary claim's, and would disturb a shipped, tuned layout for no argumentative gain. For the same reason there is **no third headline card and no creation-diet-weighted expected PPS scalar** in v2.0: creation is the why, not a third axis of the ADR-0016 decomposition — inventing a second decomposition before the data proves the need would be machinery for its own sake.

**Why no inclusion gate for contexts:** zones could be excluded from the mix view because the mix view is not a partition display; a context family **is** one — its contexts sum to the (attributed) season attempts, and dropping a low-volume context silently corrupts every displayed share and the league comparison with it. So the v1 discipline translates as: families render whole (muted-never-deleted, taken all the way), small samples are **flagged** on the conversion axis with the shared constant so † means one thing everywhere on the page, and a zero-attempt context shows its share and makes no PPS claim (ADR-0013's "no data ≠ at league average," ported).

**Why no new color scale:** the making palette means "FG% delta vs league" and nothing else — ADR-0013's fixed bins are what let the same color mean the same delta everywhere, and reusing the arms for a different unit (creation PPS deltas) would break exactly that. A creation-value encoding would need its own fixed-bin scale contract with its own guarded palette (the ADR-0014 pattern); v2.0 deliberately doesn't pay that until the panel proves it needs color at all. Likewise **no tooltips and no detail cards**: the table already carries the full story, nothing is hover-only (ADR-0027's lesson), and the bars are a presentation mapping over aggregation outputs (ADR-0011) with direct labels.

**Consequences:** `hero:report` grows a creation section (creation diet vs league, PPS by context, the unattributed count, flags) — the why-sentence is written from it, keeping the hero-swap recipe intact. When defender distance ships, it is a third group in the same chart and table, not a new surface. A future contributor must not add an FG%/eFG% column to the creation table (PPS is the unit — ADR-0001), must not color the bars by the making palette, and must not "complete the partition" by hiding small contexts.
