# Unassisted is not synonymous with self-created

An unassisted make means only that the official scorer credited no assist. v2.5 may report assisted and unassisted made-shot shares, but product and verdict copy may not translate unassisted into self-created, solo, without teammate help, or any percentage of offense the shooter created alone.

Screens, advantage passes, offensive rebounds, handoffs, and defensive rotations can create a make without producing an official assist. Case 3 assist credit is therefore one complementary creation lens, not a replacement for Case 2 catch/pull-up, clock, and defender contexts; the verdict lexicon and claim guards must preserve that distinction.
