# The headline making number is actual PPS minus diet-weighted expected PPS

The making axis gets a headline scalar as the dual of the selection number (`making.actualPps` / `making.makingPpsDelta` in `ShotMetrics`): actual points-per-shot over evaluation attempts, minus the diet-weighted expected PPS (his zone shares × league PPS). The three headline quantities decompose exactly: **league diet PPS + selection delta + making PPS delta = actual PPS**.

**Why:** the two axes must be comparable to share a headline row, so making is priced in the same unit and the same weighting as selection — PPS, over his own diet. Holding the diet fixed is what keeps the axes orthogonal: selection asks "what would he score converting like the league from where he shoots?", making asks "what did his actual conversion add or subtract from there?". Alternatives rejected: an FG%-based rollup (collapses the 3-vs-2 point difference — the failure ADR-0001 exists to prevent) and an unweighted average of per-zone making deltas (ignores the diet, so a 5-attempt zone would weigh like a 177-attempt zone). The decomposition identity is the payoff: the verdict can say "league diet 1.09, his choices +0.01, his conversion −0.11 → actual 0.99" and the numbers reassemble exactly (guarded in `aggregate.test.ts`).

**Consequences:** per-zone `makingDelta` stays FG% percentage points (one zone's conversion quality); the headline `makingPpsDelta` is PPS (the whole-diet value consequence) — the units differ on purpose; do not "unify" them. Both live in `aggregateShotMetrics` per ADR-0011. The same extension adds the **combined-threes rollup** (`threes`): player makes/attempts and league FGM/FGA summed across the three 3PT evaluation zones (the ADR-0004 rule), because the verdict's "cold from three" claim must be stated at a grain the sample supports — every launch-hero 3PT zone is individually under the 50-attempt flag (49/34/48) while the combined 131 attempts clear it. Everything here is evaluation-grain: Backcourt heaves stay excluded and separately reported, exactly as for selection.
