# The growth coda is a season-pair dumbbell on the diet axis over a spine stat line

The coda's visual reuses the product's one comparison grammar — the dumbbell, two dots on one positional axis — with the pair reinterpreted: per evaluation zone, the two dots are the two **seasons**, on a **diet-share-gap-vs-league axis measured in share points** (his share minus the league's, each season against its own league). The two-axis spine movement (selection Δ and making PPS Δ per season) renders beneath as a border-separated stat line — THE LINE's stat-coda pattern (ADR-0056 as amended). The table twin carries both seasons' full zone grain: diet share vs lg share, making Δ, † flags. The section opens in the section-title recipe with the title **SEASON OVER SEASON** and deliberately no act number; its one-line structural description names the two seasons and the framing. The coda carries no authored prose.

**Why:** chart the stable axis. Attempt shares stabilize by ~34 attempts while per-zone conversion stays †-flagged to 50, so early in a live season diet movement is signal and making movement is flag city — the chart takes the axis that reads true from flip day and the table holds the flagged one honestly. One grammar product-wide: a slopegraph would be a second comparison grammar doing the dumbbell's job; paired mini zone-courts would need a diet palette, and the making palette belongs to the making axis alone (ADR-0014); table-only would break the acts' visual-plus-twin rhythm. Share points, not ratios, on the axis: a ratio distorts a linear positional encoding, while ratio language ("double the league share") stays verdict vocabulary, guarded like any claim. The title is season-pair-agnostic structural copy: YEAR TWO breaks the moment a pair is not rookie-to-sophomore, and the acts' definite-article register (THE WHERE, THE LINE) is reserved for cuts of one season.

**Consequences:** no new palette and no new chart component class — the dumbbell's pair semantics gain a second documented meaning (entity-vs-league in the acts, season-vs-season in the coda), and the season dots need a legend the acts' his-vs-league pairs never did. The description, not the title, carries the season labels, so the title never changes on a flip. The coda's gate (ADR-0061) guarantees every evaluation zone clears the inclusion bar on both sides — gate-pass triggers the flip, and the prior side is an argued season — so the dumbbell never needs an under-bar special case; † stays a table concern on the making columns. Movement figures shown beside their two season anchors obey ADR-0023: the difference of the displayed anchors, never the rounded raw delta.
